CUSTOMER ANALYSIS PROJECT — KEY FINDINGS
==========================================

DATASET: 5,630 E-commerce Customers

1. CHURN RATE
   - 16.8% of customers churned (948 out of 5,630)
   - Industry average is 20-30%, so this is relatively healthy
   - Recommendation: Focus on reducing churn below 10%

2. TENURE INSIGHT
   - Customers in first 0-2 months have highest churn
   - Customers with 15+ months tenure almost never churn
   - Recommendation: Invest heavily in onboarding new customers

3. SATISFACTION SCORE
   - Churn happens across ALL satisfaction scores
   - Even score 4-5 customers churn
   - Recommendation: Satisfaction alone is not enough

4. RFM SEGMENTS
   - Champions:  400  (7%)  — protect these at all costs
   - Loyal:    2,360  (42%) — upsell opportunities
   - At Risk:  2,426  (43%) — urgent re-engagement needed
   - Lost:       444  (8%)  — win-back campaign needed

5. CHURN PREDICTION MODEL
   - Logistic Regression: 89% accuracy
   - Catches 46% of churners before they leave
   - Recommendation: Improve with Random Forest for better recall

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
=========================
1. Launch onboarding email series for customers in month 0-2
2. Create loyalty rewards for At Risk segment (43% of base!)
3. Run win-back campaign for 444 Lost customers
4. Deploy churn model to flag at-risk customers weekly
